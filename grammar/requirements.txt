Present Tenses
Tense Formula Use Example
Simple Present S + V1(s/es) Facts, habits People use smartphones daily.
Present Continuous S + am/is/are + V-ing Happening now People are using smartphones now.
Present Perfect S + has/have + V3 Past with present result Technology has changed society.
Present Perfect Continuous S + has/have been + V-ing Ongoing since past Technology has been changing society.


Past Tenses
Tense Formula Use Example
Simple Past S + V2 Finished past action The policy failed in 2019.
Past Continuous S + was/were + V-ing Past action in progress People were protesting yesterday.
Past Perfect S + had + V3 Before another past action The damage had occurred earlier.
Past Perfect Continuous S + had been + V-ing Duration before past point They had been waiting for hours.
Future & Conditional


Type Formula Use Example
Simple Future S + will + V1 Prediction/decision AI will transform jobs.
Be Going To S + am/is/are + going to + V1 Planned future The company is going to expand.
Second Conditional If + past, would + V1 Hypothetical If the government invested more, literacy would improve.
